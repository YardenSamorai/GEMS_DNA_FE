# GEMS DNA — Salesman Guide

A step-by-step guide for sales reps: from the moment you receive your invitation,
through logging in, to using the **Sales catalog** and your **Dashboard** every day.

> This guide covers only what you need as a salesman — the catalog and your
> dashboard. Other areas (CRM, Team management, etc.) are not part of your daily
> workflow and are not covered here.

---

## 1. Getting access (your invitation)

Access is **invite-only** — you cannot register on your own. Your manager sends
you an invitation by email.

1. Open the invitation email and tap the **link / button** inside it.
2. The app opens with a **"Create your account"** sheet that slides up from the
   bottom. Your **email is already filled in and locked** (it shows
   *"This is the email your invitation was sent to."*) — you don't need to change it.
3. Fill in:
   - **Username** — choose your own. At least 4 characters; letters, numbers or
     underscores.
   - **Password** — choose a password (tap the eye icon to show/hide it).
4. Complete the quick **"I'm not a robot"** check if it appears.
5. Tap **Continue**.
6. If asked, enter the **6-digit verification code** sent to your email, then tap
   **Verify & continue**.

That's it — your account is created and you're signed in.

> **Tip:** You can also tap **Continue with Google** instead of choosing a
> password, if your invitation email matches your Google account.

If you ever open the app **without** an invitation, you'll see an **"Access
Required"** screen. Use the **Request access** button there and your manager will
send you an invite.

---

## 2. Signing in (returning users)

When you come back later, open the app and use the **Sign in** sheet:

- **Email or username** + **Password**, then tap **Continue**, **or**
- Tap **Sign in with a passkey** (Face ID / Touch ID / device PIN) for the
  fastest, password-free login, **or**
- Tap **Continue with Google**.

**One device at a time:** your account can be active on **one device only**. If
you sign in somewhere new while still logged in elsewhere, you'll be asked to
confirm — tap **"Continue here & sign out the other device."** The old session is
then closed automatically.

---

## 3. Install it like an app (recommended)

For the best experience, add GEMS DNA to your phone's home screen so it opens
full-screen like a native app (with the **ESHED** icon):

- **iPhone (Safari):** tap the **Share** button → **Add to Home Screen** → **Add**.
- **Android (Chrome):** tap the **⋮** menu → **Add to Home screen / Install app**.

---

## 4. Finding your way around

At the bottom of the screen you have a navigation **dock**. While you're in the
sales catalog it shows these tabs:

| Tab | What it opens |
| --- | --- |
| **Dashboard** | Your home — what you've shared and your activity. |
| **Diamond** | The diamonds catalog. |
| **Emeralds** | The emeralds catalog. |
| **Gemstones** | All other colored stones. |
| **Jewelry** | Finished jewelry pieces. |
| **More** | Extra options, including **Dark / Light mode**. |

> Tapping **More** also shows who you're signed in as and lets you switch between
> **light** and **dark** appearance.

---

## 5. Your Dashboard

Open the **Dashboard** tab. You'll see:

- A personal greeting: **"Hi [Your name] 👋"**.
- Two summary cards:
  - **Sent** — total number of shares you've made.
  - **Stones** — how many unique pieces you've shared.
- **Your sends** — a list of everything you've shared with clients. Each row shows
  the stone/piece and when you sent it. **Tap any row** to jump straight to that
  item's page.

If you haven't shared anything yet, you'll see *"No stones sent yet — Share a
stone to WhatsApp from the catalog and it'll show up here."*

---

## 6. Browsing the catalog

Pick a category from the dock (**Diamond / Emeralds / Gemstones / Jewelry**).
Stones are shown as cards in a grid. Each card shows a photo and the key details,
for example:

- **Diamonds:** title (weight · shape · color · clarity · lab · fluorescence),
  cut/polish/symmetry, measurements + ratio, **Branch**, stock number, and price.
- **Emeralds & gemstones:** title, measurements, ratio, **Branch**, stock number,
  and price.

Scroll down and more stones load automatically (infinite scroll).

> **No photo?** A stone without a picture shows the **ESHED** logo as a
> placeholder. This is internal only — it is **never** sent in a WhatsApp message.

**Status tags** may appear on a card:

- **HOLD** — the stone is currently held.
- **MEMO OUT** — the stone is physically out with a third party.
- **IN JEWELRY** — the stone is set in a finished jewelry piece. **Tap this tag**
  to jump straight to that jewelry item's page.

---

## 7. Searching & scanning

At the top of any catalog you have a **search bar**:

- Type a **SKU / stock number** into **Search SKU** to find a specific stone.
- Tap the **camera icon** inside the search bar to **scan** a barcode/QR with your
  phone's rear camera — the SKU fills in automatically.

---

## 8. Filtering & sorting

**Filter** (top-left button): opens a sheet where you can narrow the list by the
fields relevant to that category — for example shape, color, clarity, weight,
price, lab, origin, treatment, branch, and more.

- Below the toolbar you'll always see a live **results count** (e.g. "128 results").
- When any filter is active, a **Reset** button appears — tap it to clear all
  filters.
- As you scroll down, a floating **Filter** button stays within reach at the
  bottom-left.

**Sort** (top-right button): opens the sort sheet. Options include **Price per
ct, Total price, Rap price, Shape, Size, Color, Clarity**.

- You can pick **more than one** — they apply in the order you tap them
  (first tap = primary sort). Tap an option again to flip its direction
  (ascending / descending).

---

## 9. Opening a stone (product page)

Tap any card to open its full page. Here you'll find:

- **Media** at the top — photos, and where available a **360° video** you can play.
- **Title** — the full description of the stone.
- **Tags row** (just under the title): **HOLD**, **Memo out**, **In Jewelry**,
  **Certificate**, and **V360**.
  - **Certificate** opens the lab certificate.
  - **V360** plays the 360° video.
  - **In Jewelry** jumps to the linked jewelry piece.
- **Spec list** — a clean list of the stone's details:
  - **Diamonds:** SKU, Polish, Sym., L/W/D (mm), Ratio, Depth, Table, Cut
    (if available), Branch, Location.
  - **Emeralds:** SKU, Origin, L/W/D (mm), L/W Ratio, Branch, Location.
  - **Other gemstones:** SKU, Color, Origin, L/W/D (mm), L/W Ratio, Branch, Location.
- A **price panel** (PPC / Rap / Price) — depending on your permissions.

> **Star button:** tap the ☆ star (top-right of the page, or the ✚ on a card) to
> add the stone to your **Selected** list (see the next section). Tap again to
> remove it.

---

## 10. Building a selection (shortlist)

Use the **star / ✚** on any stone to collect the pieces you're interested in:

- A green **Selected (N)** button appears at the bottom-right showing how many
  you've picked.
- Tap it to open the **Selected** sheet and review all your chosen stones together.
- Tap any stone there to open its page (and share it).
- Tap **Clear** to empty the list.

Your selection is saved on your device, so it stays even if you switch categories.

---

## 11. Sharing to WhatsApp

This is the core of your daily work. From a stone's page:

1. Tap the **Share / Action** button to open the share sheet.
2. Choose your options:
   - **Include prices** — toggle on/off whether the message shows the price
     (per-carat & total).
   - **Include Rap %** *(diamonds only — not fancy)* — toggle on to add a
     **Rap %** line just above the price.
3. Check the **Preview** at the bottom — it shows exactly what will be sent.
4. Tap **Share on WhatsApp**. On a phone, the stone's **photo and certificate**
   are attached automatically when supported, along with the text and links.

**What the message contains** (per stone):

- Title line (weight, shape, color/clarity, lab, etc.)
- Ratio
- **Branch** (the message shows the **branch only** — never the exact internal
  location)
- SKU
- Rap % *(only if you turned it on, diamonds)*
- Price per carat and total *(only if "Include prices" is on)*
- Links to the **video**, **certificate**, and **image** when available

Every share you make is recorded and shows up under **Your sends** on the
Dashboard.

---

## 12. What you can and can't see

Some details depend on the permissions your manager set for you:

- **Prices** may be shown or hidden for your account.
- **Internal cost** is **never** shown to salesmen (it's manager/owner only).
- **Location** shows the **Branch** on cards and in shared messages. The precise
  internal location appears on the product page only, according to your access
  level.

If you think you should see something you don't, ask your manager to update your
permissions.

---

## 13. Quick troubleshooting

- **A stone shows the ESHED logo instead of a photo** — that piece simply has no
  picture yet. It will never be sent as an image in a share.
- **"Re-run" / filters didn't carry over** — pull to refresh or reopen the
  category from the dock.
- **Can't open a section / tab is missing** — that area isn't enabled for your
  role. Ask your manager.
- **Signed out unexpectedly** — you (or someone) signed in to your account on
  another device. Just sign back in.
- **Camera scan won't open** — make sure you allowed camera access for the app in
  your phone settings.

---

### Daily workflow at a glance

1. Open the app → **Dashboard** to see your recent sends.
2. Go to the right **category** (Diamond / Emeralds / Gemstones / Jewelry).
3. **Search**, **scan**, **filter**, or **sort** to find the right stone.
4. Open the stone, review media and specs.
5. **Star** the ones you like to build a shortlist.
6. **Share on WhatsApp** with or without prices.
7. Track everything you sent under **Your sends**.

Welcome aboard — happy selling! 💎
