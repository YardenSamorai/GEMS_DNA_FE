# GEMS DNA — Zoom Walkthrough (read-aloud script)

> Read this out loud, top to bottom. **[Brackets]** are cues for you, not to read.

---

**[Share your screen.]**

Hey everyone, thanks for jumping on. I'm going to walk you through the app real
quick — by the end you'll know how to find a stone and send it to a client.

**Getting in.** You don't sign up yourself — I send you an invite by email. Open
it, tap the link, and a "Create your account" window pops up from the bottom.
Your email's already filled in, so just pick a username and a password, tick the
"I'm not a robot" box if it shows, and hit **Continue**. If it asks for a
six-digit code, that's in your email too. Done.

Next time you log in, the fastest way is **Sign in with a passkey** — Face ID or
fingerprint, no password. There's also email/password and **Continue with
Google**.

One note: you can only be logged in on one device at a time. Log in somewhere
new and it just asks you to confirm — nothing breaks.

**[Tip.]** Add it to your home screen so it opens like a real app — iPhone:
Share → "Add to Home Screen." Android: menu → "Install app."

**[Pause: "Everyone got their invite?"]**

---

**[Click into the app.]**

At the bottom you've got your tabs: **Dashboard**, then your categories —
**Diamond, Emeralds, Gemstones, Jewelry** — and **More** (that's also where you
switch light/dark mode).

**[Tap Dashboard.]** This is your home base. It greets you by name, shows how
many stones you've **Sent**, and lists **Your sends** — every stone you've
shared. Tap any of them to jump back to that stone. Starts empty until you send
your first one.

**[Tap into Diamond.]** Here are the stones — a grid of cards with the photo,
the key details, the branch, the stock number, and the price. The other
categories look the same.

You'll see tags on some cards: **HOLD** (on hold), **MEMO OUT** (physically out
with someone), and **IN JEWELRY** — tap that one and it jumps to the finished
piece the stone is set in. And if you see our ESHED logo instead of a photo,
that stone just doesn't have a picture yet — it never gets sent to a client.

**[Scroll.]** Just keep scrolling — more stones load on their own.

**Finding a stone.** Top search bar — type a stock number into **Search SKU**,
or tap the camera icon to scan a barcode. **[Tap Filter.]** **Filter** (top
left) narrows by shape, color, clarity, weight, price, and so on, with a live
result count. **[Tap Sort.]** **Sort** (top right) — price, size, color, and
more; tap again to flip the direction.

---

**[Open one stone.]**

Here's the full page: photos up top (with a 360 video if there is one), the
title, a row of tags — Certificate, V360, In Jewelry — then the full spec sheet
and the price.

**[Tap the star.]** This star adds the stone to your **Selected** list — your
shortlist for a client. **[Tap the green Selected button.]** Down here, the
green **Selected** button shows everything you've shortlisted in one place. It
stays saved on your phone, and there's **Clear** to start over.

---

**[Open a stone and tap Share.]**

Sending a stone: hit **Share**. You get a couple of switches — **Include
prices** on or off, and on regular diamonds an **Include Rap %** switch. The
**preview** at the bottom is exactly what gets sent, so you always know before
you hit send. Tap **Share on WhatsApp** and it attaches the photo and
certificate automatically.

The message shows the **branch**, never our exact internal location — that's
handled for you. And every share lands back on your Dashboard under "Your
sends."

---

**[Wrap up.]**

That's the whole thing: open the app, find the stone with search or filter, open
it, star the ones you like, and share to WhatsApp.

Last thing — if a price or a section looks hidden, that's just your permissions;
tell me and I'll fix it.

Go send yourselves a few test stones to get the feel, and ping me with
questions. Let's go sell. 💎

**[Open the floor for questions.]**
